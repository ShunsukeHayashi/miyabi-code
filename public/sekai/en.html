Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure data security and compliance?
PII is auto-masked, all data is encrypted, and managed on ISO 27001-compliant infrastructure. Complete audit logs are maintained, and we comply with GDPR and local data protection laws.
How do you integrate with existing systems (Lark, Slack, SAP, etc.)?
We support REST API, Webhooks, and OAuth2.0, with standard connectors for major business systems enabling same-day connection. For custom systems, we can integrate within 2-3 business days with API documentation.
What if the PoC doesn't succeed?
During the expectation alignment phase, we create a risk/ROI agreement with clear success criteria. If PoC goals aren't met, we implement improvement measures at no additional cost, or offer a refund option (based on contract terms).
How much operational resource is needed?
Initial deployment requires 5-10 hours/week from key personnel (1-2 operations managers). During operations phase, only 1-2 hours/week for reviews, with Strike Team leading implementation.
How does expectation alignment work?
Through dedicated workshops on Day 0-14, we visualize operational challenges, set KPIs, and share risks. By avoiding unrealistic expectations and setting practical milestones, we target certain results within 90 days.
What about international locations and multilingual support?
Standard support for Japanese, English, Chinese, and Korean. Through ByteDance/Lark integration, global locations can operate on the same platform.

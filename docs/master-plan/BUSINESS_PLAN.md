**Strategy**: Monetize enterprise features

- **Revenue**: $100K-500K

  - Pro: $49/month/user (teams <10)

### Phase 4: Platform Ecosystem (2030+)
**Strategy**: Marketplace and partnerships

- **Revenue**: $2M+

# Discord Community Launch & Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary

**Mission**: Launch the world's premier Discord community for reality-based AI agent development, achieving 5,000+ members and 500+ active contributors within 6 months.

**Strategy**: Multi-phase launch combining organic growth through value-driven content, strategic partnerships, and targeted marketing to developer and business audiences.

**Unique Value Proposition**: The only Discord community focused exclusively on production-ready AI agent implementations with direct access to framework creators and expert practitioners.

## Launch Strategy Overview

### Phase 1: Foundation & Soft Launch (Weeks 1-4)
**Objective**: Build core infrastructure and validate community-market fit with initial user base
**Target**: 500-1,000 engaged members
**Focus**: Product community, GitHub followers, existing customers

### Phase 2: Content-Driven Growth (Weeks 5-12)
**Objective**: Scale through valuable content and community programming
**Target**: 2,000-3,000 members with high engagement
**Focus**: Developer community, content marketing, influencer partnerships

### Phase 3: Partnership & Scale (Weeks 13-20)
**Objective**: Accelerate growth through strategic partnerships and enterprise outreach
**Target**: 4,000-5,000 members with diverse user base
**Focus**: Ecosystem partners, enterprise prospects, industry events

### Phase 4: Sustainability & Optimization (Weeks 21-26)
**Objective**: Optimize for long-term sustainability and community leadership
**Target**: 5,000+ members with 500+ active contributors
**Focus**: Community-driven growth, self-sustaining engagement, thought leadership

## Phase 1: Foundation & Soft Launch

### Week 1-2: Infrastructure Setup

#### Server Configuration
- Complete Discord server setup with all 8 categories and 44 channels
- Configure permission system for all role tiers
- Implement bot integrations (GitHub, welcome, analytics, moderation)
- Create and populate initial content in key channels

#### Content Creation
- **Welcome Package**: Complete onboarding flow and materials
- **Rules & Guidelines**: Community standards and moderation policies
- **Getting Started Resources**: Tutorials, documentation links, example projects
- **FAQ Database**: Anticipate and answer common questions
- **Event Calendar**: Schedule first month of programming

#### Team Preparation
- Train moderation team on community guidelines and tools
- Prepare Miyabi team for increased community engagement
- Create response templates for common questions and scenarios
- Establish community management workflows and responsibilities

### Week 3-4: Private Beta Launch

#### Target Audience (Initial 500-1,000 members)
- **Existing Customers**: Current Miyabi users and trial participants
- **GitHub Community**: Followers and contributors to Miyabi repositories
- **Internal Network**: Team connections, advisors, and industry contacts
- **Beta Testers**: Previous beta participants and power users

#### Launch Activities
- **Personal Invitations**: Direct invites to VIP community members
- **Email Campaign**: Announcement to existing customer and subscriber lists
- **GitHub Integration**: Automatic invites to repository contributors
- **Soft Launch Event**: "Community Foundation Day" with team introductions

#### Success Metrics
- **Member Acquisition**: 500-1,000 members in 2 weeks
- **Engagement Rate**: 60%+ of invited members complete onboarding
- **Content Interaction**: 70%+ of members engage with initial content
- **Retention**: 80%+ of beta members remain active after 2 weeks

### Beta Launch Content Strategy

#### Week 3: Foundation Content
**Monday**: Server launch announcement and welcome video from founders
**Wednesday**: "Building Your First Agent" workshop for beta community
**Friday**: Community Q&A and feedback session with leadership team

#### Week 4: Value Demonstration

#### Beta Feedback Collection
- **Member Experience Survey**: Onboarding, navigation, content quality
- **Technical Content Assessment**: Workshop effectiveness, documentation clarity

- **Programming Preferences**: Event timing, format preferences, topic priorities

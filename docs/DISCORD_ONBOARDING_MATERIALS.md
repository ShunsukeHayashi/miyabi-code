## Progressive Engagement Strategy

### 7-Day New Member Journey

#### Day 1: Welcome & Orientation
**Automated Actions:**
- Welcome DM sent
- @New Member role assigned
- Introduction to #welcome channel
- Guided channel tour via bot

**Community Goals:**
- Complete email verification
- Read community guidelines
- Post introduction in #introductions
- Join at least one relevant channel

#### Day 2-3: First Interactions
**Automated Follow-up:**
- Check-in DM: "How's your first day going?"
- Highlight upcoming events
- Suggest relevant channels based on introduction

**Community Goals:**
- Ask first question or participate in discussions
- React to and engage with others' content
- Explore project showcase channels
- Bookmark useful resources

#### Day 4-5: Deeper Engagement
**Automated Support:**
- Share personalized resource recommendations
- Invite to upcoming beginner-friendly events
- Highlight ways to contribute and get recognized

**Community Goals:**
- Participate in office hours or workshop
- Contribute meaningful content (answer, insight, resource)
- Connect with other community members
- Share feedback or suggestions

#### Day 6-7: Integration Assessment
**Automated Check:**
- "How has your first week been?" survey
- Graduation to @Member role (if active)
- Recognition for quality contributions

**Community Goals:**
- Feel integrated into community discussions
- Have received helpful answers to questions
- See clear path for continued engagement
- Understand community value proposition

### 30-Day Active Member Path

#### Week 1: Foundation Building
**Milestones:**
- Complete onboarding successfully
- Make 10+ meaningful contributions
- Attend at least one community event
- Connect with 3+ other members

#### Week 2: Knowledge Sharing
**Milestones:**
- Answer questions in help channels
- Share useful resources or tools
- Participate in technical discussions
- Provide feedback on others' projects

#### Week 3: Community Leadership
**Milestones:**
- Help onboard newer members
- Lead or contribute to discussions
- Share your own project or work
- Provide valuable feedback and insights

#### Week 4: Recognition & Growth
**Assessment Criteria:**
- Consistent positive community participation
- Quality of contributions and helpfulness
- Leadership in discussions and helping others
- Zero community guideline violations

**Promotion to @Active Member:**
- Priority in help channels
- Beta testing access
- Special recognition badge
- Input on community direction

### Long-term Engagement Pathways

#### Contributor Track (GitHub Integration)
**Path to @Contributor Role:**
1. Make meaningful code contributions to Miyabi projects
2. Provide quality code reviews for community contributions
3. Maintain helpful and constructive community participation
4. Demonstrate deep technical knowledge through discussions

**Benefits:**
- Contributor badge and recognition
- Early access to new features
- Input on technical roadmap
- Enhanced community moderation abilities

#### Expert Track (Knowledge Leadership)
**Path to @Core Contributor Role:**
1. Consistently provide expert-level technical assistance
2. Create valuable educational content (tutorials, guides)
3. Mentor other community members effectively
4. Lead community initiatives and discussions

**Benefits:**
- Expert recognition and platform
- Direct communication with development team
- Speaking opportunities at events
- Community leadership responsibilities

#### Professional Track (Subscription-Based)
**@Professional User Benefits:**
- Access to professional-only channels
- Priority support across all channels
- Advanced feature discussions and beta access
- Professional networking opportunities

#### Enterprise Track (Commercial Focus)
**@Enterprise Customer Benefits:**
- Enterprise-specific channels and discussions
- Direct team communication channels
- Priority feature requests and roadmap input
- Dedicated enterprise support and resources

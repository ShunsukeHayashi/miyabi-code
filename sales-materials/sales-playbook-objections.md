## Sales Methodology Overview

### The Miyabi Sales Framework: "PROVEN"

#### **P - Problem Identification**
```yaml
Objective: Understand the customer's current pain points and AI challenges
Key Activities:
  - Identify failed AI implementations and their costs
  - Understand resource constraints and budget pressures
  - Discover compliance and security requirements
  - Assess competitive threats and time-to-market pressure

Success Criteria:
  - Clear understanding of business pain points
  - Quantified impact of current problems
  - Identified decision-makers and budget authority
  - Established urgency and priority for solution
```

#### **R - Reality Assessment**
```yaml
Objective: Evaluate the customer's current state and readiness for change
Key Activities:
  - Assess technical infrastructure and capabilities
  - Understand organizational change readiness
  - Evaluate past vendor relationships and experiences
  - Identify success criteria and measurement methods

Success Criteria:
  - Realistic assessment of implementation requirements
  - Understanding of organizational dynamics
  - Clear picture of decision-making process
  - Identified risks and mitigation strategies
```

#### **O - Opportunity Mapping**
```yaml
Objective: Connect Miyabi capabilities to specific business opportunities
Key Activities:
  - Map specific use cases to business value
  - Quantify potential ROI and cost savings
  - Identify expansion and scaling opportunities
  - Connect to strategic business initiatives

Success Criteria:
  - Clear alignment between needs and capabilities
  - Quantified business case with specific metrics
  - Identified quick wins and long-term value
  - Connected to executive priorities and initiatives
```

#### **V - Value Demonstration**
```yaml
Objective: Prove Miyabi's ability to deliver specific business outcomes
Key Activities:
  - Deliver targeted demonstrations of relevant capabilities
  - Present customer references and case studies
  - Conduct proof-of-concept with measurable outcomes
  - Provide detailed implementation and success plans

Success Criteria:
  - Customer confidence in solution capabilities
  - Validation of proposed approach and timeline
  - Stakeholder alignment on expected outcomes
  - Clear understanding of implementation path
```

#### **E - Engagement Acceleration**
```yaml
Objective: Drive decision-making and commitment to move forward
Key Activities:
  - Address objections and concerns proactively
  - Facilitate stakeholder alignment and consensus
  - Create urgency through limited-time offers or competitive pressure
  - Provide clear next steps and decision framework

Success Criteria:
  - All major objections addressed and resolved
  - Decision-maker commitment to evaluation timeline
  - Internal champion advocacy and support
  - Clear path to purchase decision
```

#### **N - Negotiation & Close**
```yaml
Objective: Finalize agreement terms and execute contract
Key Activities:
  - Negotiate pricing and contract terms
  - Address final concerns and implementation details
  - Secure legal and procurement approval
  - Plan implementation kickoff and success measurement

Success Criteria:
  - Mutually acceptable contract terms agreed
  - All stakeholder approvals obtained
  - Implementation plan and timeline confirmed
  - Customer success transition completed
```

## Objection Handling Guide

### Price and Budget Objections

#### **"The price is higher than our budget."**

**Discovery Questions:**
- "What budget range were you expecting for an enterprise AI platform?"
- "How did you arrive at that budget estimate?"
- "What's the cost of not having an AI platform in place?"
- "How much have you invested in failed AI projects previously?"

**Response Framework:**
```yaml
Acknowledge & Understand:
  "I understand budget is a critical consideration. Let me make sure I understand
  your budget constraints and see if there's a way to make this work within your requirements."

Reframe the Investment:
  "Rather than thinking about this as a cost, let's look at it as an investment.
  Our ROI analysis shows that most customers save more in the first quarter than
  the entire first year investment. Based on your situation, you'd save approximately
  $[X] per month, which means the platform pays for itself in [Y] months."

Provide Options:
  "Let me present a few options that might work better for your budget:
  1. Phased implementation starting with highest-ROI use cases
  2. Financing options to spread the investment over multiple quarters
  3. Enterprise Starter tier with upgrade path as you scale"

Create Urgency:
  "The cost of waiting is significant. Every month you delay, you're missing out
  on $[X] in savings and efficiency gains. Plus, your competitors are moving
  ahead with AI automation."
```

**Supporting Materials:**
- Custom ROI calculator showing payback period
- Financing and payment term options
- Phased implementation plan with staged costs
- Competitive cost comparison analysis

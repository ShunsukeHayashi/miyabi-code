## Executive Summary

This document outlines the primary B2B customer personas for Miyabi AI Agent Framework, focusing on enterprise decision-makers who can drive significant revenue growth. Our analysis identifies three core personas across organizations with 100-10,000 employees, representing a $2.4B total addressable market.

**Key Findings**:
- 73% of enterprise AI projects fail due to inadequate frameworks
- Average enterprise customer lifetime value: $485,000
- 18-month average sales cycle for enterprise AI solutions
- Primary buying triggers: Failed previous AI implementations, compliance requirements, cost reduction mandates
